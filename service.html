Our Services

Services That We Offers

Solar Energy Solutions

We design and install solar power systems tailored to homes, businesses, and communities. Our team ensures optimal panel placement, system efficiency, and long-term reliability. Whether you're looking to cut energy costs or achieve energy independence, our solar solutions are built for maximum impact.

Wind Energy Projects

Harnessing the power of wind, we deliver scalable wind turbine solutions for rural electrification and industrial applications. Our wind systems are designed to perform efficiently in Nigeria’s diverse wind zones.

Energy Storage Systems

Energy storage is at the core of reliable renewable power. We provide advanced battery systems that store excess energy for use during cloudy days, nights, or outages—ensuring an uninterrupted power supply..

Project Development & Consulting

From feasibility studies to full project execution, VoltRoot offers end-to-end support for renewable energy development. We work with government agencies, NGOs, and private investors to bring projects to life.

Community Electrification

We specialize in off-grid energy systems that bring electricity to underserved and remote communities. Our mini-grid and solar home system programs empower households, schools, and health centers.
